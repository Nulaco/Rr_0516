About Hardwork

　　Qingong Hardwork Co., Ltd. was established in Taiwan in 1980. For more than 20 years, it mainly produces seamless sliding contact line safety rails and peripheral accessories. With its best quality, reasonable price and perfect after-sales service, it has won the favor of the crane industry. In 2006, our company set up a branch company in Dongguan, Dongguan Qingong Plastic Hardware Co., Ltd., to facilitate the crane equipment factory in Mainland China and the need to expand production.
　　Dongguan Qingong Plastic & Hardware Co., Ltd. inherits Taiwan Qingong's consistent management, business philosophy and quality policy, strives for excellence in production technology, requires fair price, strives for customer satisfaction in after-sales service, and produces more diversified Products meet customer needs.
　　Our quality has passed the inspection of ISO9001, and our products have obtained CE certification. Therefore, the company's products are more secure in use.
　　The wise choice is the customer's rights and interests, and the reputation of the product is our responsibility.
